SimpliLearn Project 3 :Laptop Online Shop

In this phase I have made a Online laptop shop for Digital Retailers Inc which wants to 
expands its business online.

So using Asp.Net MVC i have made a online shop for them 

Here there are three models: Order,Laptop,Carousel

The site has Index page,aboutUs and contactUs page

After clicking on more details of product is shown and clicking buy will ask for 
client details fill it accordingly.

The Orders list is shown after entering the client details

or the client can continue shopping or see their orders.
